The game is simple:

you choose a mode:

to guess numbers from 0 to 10

tp guess numbers from 0 to 50

to guess a numbers from 0 to 100

and whenever you guess correctly the the result shall be saved in a score next to your
username and can be displayed to you whenever you click on "end".


note: your score and username won't be permanently saved, whenever you leave the
game your data shall be erased.
